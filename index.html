Akan Names Generator

According to the Ghanian culture, people are given their first names depending
on the day of the week they were born. Each gender has names that correspond
to the seven days of the week. This website is a way of calculating a person's
Akan name depending on the day, the month, and the year they were born

How it works

The user is supposed to enter their birthday details as prompted. Don't worry,
the day of the week in which you were born will be calculated for you too! Your
Akan name will be displayed as a pop up on the top of the page
